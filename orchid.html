❀ Orchid ❀
(Orchidaceae)

The meaning of an orchid depends on its color.

White: Innocence, purity, reverence, humility,
and elegance
Pink: Grace, femininity, happiness, joy,
and fertility
Purple: Royalty, respect, admiration, authority,
and dignity
Red: Passion, desire, strength, courage, perseverance,
and love
Yellow: Friendship, new beginnings, and joy
Green: Nature, health, good fortune, and longevity
Orange: Pride, boldness, enthusiasm, vitality, creativity,
and energy
Blue: Beauty, rarity, uniqueness, spirituality, confidence,
loyalty, and tranquility
Black: Mystery, power, sophistication, strength, determination,
absolute power, and authority
