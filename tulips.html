❀ Tulips ❀
(Tulipa)

Tulips can symbolize love, rebirth, and
new beginnings. The color of the tulip can
also convey different meanings.

White: Forgiveness, respect, purity, and honor
Red: Eternal love and passion
Yellow: Cheerfulness and hope
Orange: Enthusiasm, energy, and a zest for life
Pink: Affection, caring, good wishes, and love
Purple: Royalty and elegance
Blue: Tranquility, serenity, and inner peace
