❀ Lily of the Valley ❀
(Convallaria majalis)

The lily of the valley has many
symbolic meanings, including purity, humility,
sweetness, and the return of happiness.

As the birth flower of May, lily of the valley is
perfect for anyone with a May birthday! Since it's
also known as a symbol of happiness, these
blooms are a great gift for someone you care about
or who makes you smile.
